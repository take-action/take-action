How To Help

What can you do to help A21? Together, we are eradicating human trafficking through awareness, intervention, and aftercare. A21 mission is to end slavery They are a non-profit organization fuelled by radical to become the abolitionists of the 21st century. The operational strategy of A21 at the centre of the organization, is to Reach, Rescue, and Restore. REACH - REDUCING VULNERABILITY - prevent slavery from ever happening through awareness and education. RESCUE - IDENTIFYING VICTIMS -Work with law enforcement to identify victims, assist in the prosecution of traffickers, represent survivors in court proceedings, and collaborate with governments and other NGOs to eradicate slavery RESTORE - EMPOWERING SURVIVORS Work with every survivor providing them with access to housing, medical treatment, counselling, education, employment, and repatriation to help them reach a place of independence. Join us Volunteer with A21 to educate and inform the masses about the work A21 does and the problem it is trying to solve. A-Teams – act and take your place in history to abolish slavery. A-Teams are an extension of A21 and the fuel behind the mission–going to unreached places with the message of freedom. We believe that with grit, tenacity, and dedication, we can abolish slavery everywhere, forever Our A-team members are fundraising, educating others about human trafficking, participating in Walk for Freedom, putting together local initiatives, and spreading awareness through A21's campaigns. Campaigns Walk for Freedom- a global fundraising & awareness event. Show up locally, will impact globally. Walk for Freedom is a global fundraising and awareness event. Rallying thousands of abolitionists, taking millions of steps, showing up in hundreds of cities all over the world, Walk for Freedom is an outward expression of our inward desire to see slavery completely abolished. On October 20, 2018 in communities around the world, you’ll see us–21st century abolitionists–walking in single-file lines wearing black with a message that can't be ignored: slavery still exists. Can you see me It’s a public awareness campaign with the ultimate purpose of reaching more victims and arresting more traffickers. Our goal is to turn awareness into action by equipping people to report tips to national hotlines when they suspect trafficking, so that victims are identified and assisted. Education Education is essential to end human trafficking.
