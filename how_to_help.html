Who are A21?


Together, with people like you there mission is to eradicate human trafficking through awareness, intervention, and aftercare.

A21's mission is to end slavery.

They are a non-profit organization fuelled by radical to become the abolitionists of the 21st century.



The operational strategy of A21 at the centre of the organization, is to Reach, Rescue, and Restore.

  • EACH

    • REACH - REDUCING VULNERABILITY - prevent slavery from ever happening through awareness and education.
  • ESCUE
    • RESCUE - IDENTIFYING VICTIMS -Work with law enforcement to identify victims, assist in the prosecution of traffickers, represent survivors in court proceedings, and collaborate with governments and other NGOs to eradicate slavery
  • ESTORE
    • RESTORE - EMPOWERING SURVIVORS Work with every survivor providing them with access to housing, medical treatment, counselling, education, employment, and repatriation to help them reach a place of independence.

    Can you see me

    It’s a public awareness campaign with the ultimate purpose of reaching more victims and arresting more traffickers. Our goal is to turn awareness into action by equipping people to report tips to national hotlines when they suspect trafficking, so that victims are identified and assisted.
